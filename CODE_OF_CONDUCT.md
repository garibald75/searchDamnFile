# Code of Conduct

This project should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent, especially in bug reports and reviews.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Make room for people with different experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users who ignore
these expectations.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact a maintainer privately where
possible.
